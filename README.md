# Mines of Hormuz

A Minesweeper game set in the Strait of Hormuz, styled as a Bloomberg terminal. Sweep naval mines while oil prices react in real time to your progress.

## Features

- **Geographically accurate map** of the Strait of Hormuz with Iran, Oman, UAE coastlines, Musandam Peninsula, and islands (Qeshm, Hormuz, Larak, Hengam)
- **Live oil price ticker** — Brent Crude fluctuates based on gameplay; prices spike when you hit a mine and drop as you clear the strait
- **4 difficulty levels** — Patrol, Frigate, Destroyer, Admiral
- **Bloomberg terminal aesthetic** — dark UI with market data panels, intraday chart, news ticker, and headline alerts
- **Mobile friendly** — responsive grid, long-press to flag on touch devices
- **Zero dependencies** — single `index.html` file, no build step

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a browser
2. Click water cells to reveal them — numbers indicate adjacent mines
3. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag suspected mines
4. Clear all safe water cells to win and stabilize oil prices
5. Hit a mine and watch Brent Crude go parabolic
